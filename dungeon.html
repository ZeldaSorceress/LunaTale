Forest Temple


For the first room(right image): Proceed to where the red arrow is pointing. There is a chest with 20G along with a save point.


For the last rooms(images provided in web package): The object circled will give you restorative items. The enemy in the red square is the miniboss, you will have to fight him and his brother to proceed. After completing that, you may proceed to the place marked with the red arrow. In the last room you have to fight the boss and his minions. Before entering this room make sure you are well prepared as you cannot go back. The only way to return is to defeat the boss. Once you have defeated the boss he will drop the Blessing of Life(main collectable) and afterwards you will be safely teleported outside of the dungeon. You will then proceed to the next area.
