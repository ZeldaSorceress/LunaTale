Forest Temple


For the first room(right image): Proceed to where the red arrow is pointing. There is a chest with 20G along with a save point.

For the last rooms: The object circled will give you restorative items. The enemy in the red square is the miniboss, you will have to fight him and his brother to proceed. After completing that, you may proceed to the place marked with the red arrow. In the last room you have to fight the boss and his minions. Before entering this room make sure you are well prepared as you cannot go back. The only way to return is to defeat the boss. Once you have defeated the boss he will drop the Blessing of Life(main collectable) and afterwards you will be safely teleported outside of the dungeon. You will then proceed to the next area.

Dungeon Lore: The temple was originally guarded by the God of Life, but after a terrible war with the Wolf demon tribe the God of Life lost the temple. The God of Life struck a bargain with the leader of the Wolf demon tribe. He offered them the temple if they guarded the Blessing of Life and left the villagers alone. The leader grumbled about the offer but very quickly agreed to it. The villagers don't go into the temple because anyone who goes in there doesn't come out alive.

Weeping Cave


For the first room(right image): Upon entering this room you will see a savepoint and 2 passages. The top passage will lead you to the boss door and some treasure. The other passage will lead you to receive the boss key.

For the last rooms: You will receive the boss key by defeating the miniboss which is 2 harpies. When the harpies are defeated, one of them will tell you the secret code to receive the dungeon's special skill. Once you have completed the puzzle you will then be able to get the skill. After you have received the special skill, you will then be able to take on the boss. Upon defeating the boss you will receive the Blessing of Death(Main Collectable).

Dungeon Lore: This cave used to belong to the Goddess of Death, but because she became corrupted she lost it. She is made into the source of the corruption that plauges the land. Seeing this oppurtunity, the Harpy King decided to claim the cave as his own property. Since he has claimed it as his own personal kingdom, none are welcome. The only way to even reach the passage way into the cave is to present an Ancient Shard to the statue in the graveyard. This graveyard can be found in Weeping Village in the top left.

Champion Temple


For the first room(right image): Upon entering you will see 4 passages, but for this part of the game you will not be able to complete the room with the red curtain.

For the last rooms: You will need to find the levers to proceed to the Boss Room. These levers can be found by going down the stairs in the starting room. Each of those stairways leads you to the levers, no fighting is allowed below the stairs so don't worry. Once you have activated both levers, you should be able to pass through the door with the brown curtain. Be aware that danger lies behind this door so go in prepared.

Dungeon Lore: The Goddess of Nature resides inside this dungeon she calls home. She waits for the person to come and be her champion, hence the name "Champion Temple" each God has their own Temple and they are sworn to defend it. There is a lady in the area called Sky City which is located just outside of this Temple. This lady is the daughter of the Goddess of Nature and is entrusted with the Temple key. In order to get inside the Temple, you must get the key from her.

Golum Cave


For the first room(right image): Upon entering you will see 2 passages you can go through. You will also see a savepoint towards the right. The upper passage will lead you to the boss door and the passage on the left will take you further into the dungeon.

For the last rooms: You will need to activate the bridge in order to face the miniboss and get the Boss Key. You will have to battle some enemies along the way and it will be difficult because these enemies are immune to almost any type of magic except for Dark magic. The companion you get before entering this area will come in handy, however he is not that powerful so these battles will prove difficult.

Dungeon Lore: The Goddess of Power is supposed to protect this place but instead she leaves that job to the Dragon who dwells inside. It is rumored to believe that the Dragon who currently dwells inside the Cave actually made it himself. The Goddess of Power hasn't been seen for ages but some say she still hides in plain sight waiting for someone worthy of her to appear.

Icicilis Cave

For the first room(right image): This dungeon is the shortest dungeon in the entire game so when you enter you will only have to fight the boss.

For the last rooms:

Dungeon Lore: The Goddess of Ice protects this cave that houses the Blessing of Ice. She is the only one out of all the other Gods who doesn't have a lot of wisdom. When you reach this dungeon, the Goddess has already succumbed to the corruption and is turned into a dark God. The only thing that can save her is the person holding the Ice Shard who is not a Fox.

The Lunarium

For the first room(right image): Upon entering this dungeon you will see two passages, a savepoint, two levers and the Boss Door. The two levers unlock the Boss Door but the levers are stuck so you have to explore the dungeon a bit.

For the last rooms: The two passage ways take you to two different puzzles that unlock the levers in the first room. However the passage on the right will take you to the miniboss which will grant you the Boss Key. Even if you have the Boss Key you will still not be able to get in the Boss Chamber unless the levers are switched.

Dungeon Lore: This dungeon is the Galaxy King's palace and is the only palace in the entire Galaxy. The King built the entire palace to have a place he can relax and spend time with the woman he fell in love with. The woman he fell in love with happened to be Luna's mother and later in the dungeon you find out that the King is your father. Towards the end of the dungeon he grants you half of his remaining power so you can save your mother.

Oceana Temple

For the first room(right image): Upon entering you will see the boss door, a staircase on the left and right. You will also see some other passage ways plus a Goddess Statue.

For the last rooms: In order to access the Boss Door, you must pass the 3 trials just outside of the dungeon. You can access these trials by using the staircase on the right. Be aware that these trials are not easy. Once you have all 3 Trial Stones, you may proceed back to the starting room and go to the Magic Library. You can find the Magic Library in the starting room with the blue sigil with the glowing blue orb.Once you have successfully entered, you will be given the powerful book of blessings. In order to get this book you must have all 4 blessings that you should've collected throughout the game. Once you have used the book and acquired the skill, you may then proceed back to the starting room. Use the stairway on the left and it will take you to the Boss Key.

Crystallis Castle

For the first room(right image): When you first enter this dungeon you will encounter a cutscene that removes the Village Elder from your party. After that the Spirit of Corruption sends you to a cellblock.

For the last rooms: Once you find yourself in your cellblock, you can use the chest to get out. Once your out you will have to fight your way out of the dungeon. Eventually you will make your way back to the main room. Here you will see a savepoint and 4 passages. The passage to the south will take you out of the dungeon and the passage to the far right will take you to the boss. In order to proceed further into the dingeon, you must first go to the doorway on the top right. There you will have to fight a false god who is very powerful. After defeating him, you can proceed to the doorway on the top left. There you will fight the miniboss which is the Goddess of Death. When you have defeated the Goddess of Death, that will break almost every patch of corruption but some part of it is left behind to imprison the Queen of Gods(your mother). You may proceed to the boss chamber after defeating the Goddess of Death.
